Office Of Naval Intelligence

What is ONI

The Office of Naval Intelligence commonly shortened to ONI is the intelligence service of the United Nations Space Command Navy. Overtly, Oni is the information gathering and analysis division of the UNSC Naval Command. In reality, our operations are far broader than simple intelligence gathering.

SECTION 1

ONI SECTION 1 is the proper intelligence-gathering branch of ONI. As such, the section's services are most often utilized by UNSC forces. Its responsibilities include information gathering and codebreaking through military espionage.

Information released by Section One includes evacuation notices for colony worlds and Covenant force deployments.
