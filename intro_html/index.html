What is ONI

The Office of Naval Intelligence was founded in 2178. One of the earliest known projects undertaken by ONI was in 2321. This project, dubbed ORION, was an attempt to increase the effectiveness of Colonial Military Authority soldiers. However this initial run was eventually postponed. In 2491. the Carver Findings released and ONI began to use it as their approach to the Insurrectionists. leading to Operation: TREBUCHET Our operatives were instrumental in quelling the insurrectionist threat to the outer colonies ufotunately do to this theat we were unprepared when something far worse found us In 2517, candidates for the new Generation II ORION Project later the spartan II program were recruted from military personnel scouted in bootcamp and brought to Reach for training and later augmentation The Office of Naval Intelligence commonly shortened to ONI is the intelligence service of the United Nations Space Command Navy. Overtly, Oni is the information gathering and analysis division of the UNSC Naval Command. In reality, our operations are far broader than simple intelligence gathering. ONI in the War Against the Covenant In the first several months of the war, some ONI analysts believed the Covenant may have visited Earth in the past. Part of this was due to the meter and a half tall Unggoy the Covenant employed. As such, the Covenant received the nickname "LGM" or "Little Green Men". Over the course of the war, ONI eliminated or gained control over most competing civilian and military espionage services. for the greater good we could not have infighting durring such a volatile period. Some may find our methods extreme, but when faced with extinction, any alternative is preferable. post war Following the war, the Office of Naval Intelligence had become the primary intelligence service of the UEG. ONI was also afforded a great deal of autonomy, due to our work during the war with the UNSC Security Council. As of 2555, Admiral Margaret Parangosky was replaced with Admiral Serin Osman as Commander-in-Chief of the Office of Naval Intelligence or CINCONI.

Sections of ONI

Section-1

Section One is the proper intelligence-gathering branch of ONI. As such, the section's services are most often utilized by UNSC forces. Its responsibilities include information gathering and codebreaking through military espionage. Information released by Section One includes evacuation notices for colony worlds and Covenant force deployments. Section One is responsible for covering up all information on locations ONI wishes to keep from the public eye

Section-2

Section Two only deals with external communication and public morale.It handles communication between colonies. Section Two is also responsible for broadcasting pre-recorded human carrier signals from various locations into slipspace to confuse Covenant triangulation technology, preventing them from discovering Earth. this division was responsible for the Cole:protocal

Section-3

Section Three is the top-secret projects division of ONI that oversees the various groups responsible for creating new innovations through the use of advanced alien technology.It is responsible for the SPARTAN-IIand many other operations. Section Three's programs are well-known and are widely celebrated for their success.
